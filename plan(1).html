Goals

The new website is intended to increase the publicity and revenue of the current Fish-tastic business by exposing the new expanding businesses of a petshop and setting up koi ponds and landscapes.

Success Evaluation

Fish-tastic business will resume its day to day operations and normal advertizements as usual. After a 3 month period, Fish-tastic will evaluate the quantity of products sold for Fish-tastic and services of new businesses. The company will evalute the level of revenue after website is made and the revenue level before the new-website was developed. The successful evaluation will need 3 months, so that changes in company revenue stream will be attributed to the website, not other factors.

Target Audience

Pet enthusiasts who are either looking for a pet or is already a pet-owner. The target audience would most likely be in the range of 15 to 40 years. Despite the large age gap, the intention is that the website should be easy to use.
